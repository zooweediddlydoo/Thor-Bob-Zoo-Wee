Our Story

It all began one humid July afternoon. Two best friends realized that while life was changing fast, the joy of a dog remains constant. With one of us already a dedicated dog parent, we decided that summer was the perfect time to make a big decision. We set out to create toys and treats that were as playful as our own pets. At a young age, we learned that the best businesses start with a simple, great idea and a lot of heart.
