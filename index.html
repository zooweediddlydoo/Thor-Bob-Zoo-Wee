How We Started

In the **Summer of 2026**, a friend and I were watching his two Chihuahuas—**Thor** (a feisty boy) and **Pearl** (his graceful sister)—play in the backyard. We noticed a frustrating pattern: the toys and gear available weren't just low quality; they felt generic and disposable.

We realized our pets deserved gear that reflected the effort we put into caring for them. Instead of waiting for someone else to fix it, we decided to **take matters into our own hands**. We began sourcing and testing products that could survive the surprisingly high energy of two Chihuahuas and meet our standards for durability and design.

How You Can Help

By choosing a small business like ours, you aren't just buying a toy—you're supporting a shift away from mass-produced, impersonal products. Your support allows us to focus on quality and thoughtful curation, helping us build a future where pet businesses are run by people who actually know and love dogs.
