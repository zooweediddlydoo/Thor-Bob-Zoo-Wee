The Summer of 2026

During the summer of 2026, we realized that our own dogs were consistently underwhelmed by the toys available on the market, which often failed to hit the mark on both durability and genuine fun. We observed that most products were either indestructible but boring, or engaging but easily shredded within minutes.

Driven by the desire to provide Thor and Pearl with better options, we began prototyping our own designs that combined high-engagement textures with professional-grade materials. Today, Thor & Pearl Co. stands as a testament to that mission, ensuring every pup has access to gear that is as playful as it is permanent.
